FUTURE CITY

This website delves into the dual aspects of advantages and disadvantages that the future holds for its inhabitants, particularly concerning the upcoming construction of future cities worldwide. The swift expansion of urban areas poses a significant threat to the sustainability and overall quality of urban life. Mass urbanization can introduce social instability, potentially jeopardizing a city's ability to thrive both environmentally and economically.
